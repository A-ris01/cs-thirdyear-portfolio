# cs third year portfolio

## about this journey
the third year is known as the backbone of computer science, but for me, it was much more than just subjects. it was a test of patience and willpower.

we went through this year under extraordinary circumstances. between the echoes of war, road closures that made reaching the lecture hall an achievement in itself, and the heavy traffic that consumed our time and energy. these were heavy days, with complex programming and theoretical materials that felt endless at times.

however, there was a great human side to this struggle. thanks to god, and then the support of our professors who were more than just teachers—they were a true support system—we were able to overcome these challenges together. this repository is not just code; it is a witness that we continued learning and innovating despite everything.

## repository structure
this repository contains my academic and programming archive for this stage:

* database lec+labs: all related to database design and practical projects.
* webapplication lec+labs: web development from basics to integrated projects.
* flutter_projects: mobile applications projects.
* academic_resources: summaries for complex subjects.

## technologies used
* languages: dart (flutter), sql, javascript, html/css.
* tools: git and github, vs code, android studio.
* environment: windows 11 pro on lenovo loq.

## acknowledgment
thanks to every classmate who shared the struggle of the road, and thanks to every professor who appreciated our circumstances and was a human before being an academic. we are here today because we did not give up.
